Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Introduction

ClarifierAI ("the App") is a keyboard extension for iOS that transforms text using GPT technology. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. Your privacy is our top priority.

Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ClarifierAI:

  • Account Information: Email address collected via Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In for authentication purposes.
  • User Content: Text you submit for improvement, rephrasing, or translation. This text is collected when you actively use the keyboard extension to transform text.

Third-Party AI Service Data Sharing

Important: ClarifierAI shares your text content with OpenAI, a third-party artificial intelligence service provider, to process your transformation requests.

What Data Is Sent

When you use the App to transform text, the following data is sent to OpenAI:

  • The text content you select to improve, rephrase, or translate
  • Your transformation preferences (e.g., tone, style, language)

We do NOT send your email address, device information, or any other personal identifiers to OpenAI.

Who Receives Your Data

Your text content is sent to OpenAI (OpenAI, L.L.C., based in San Francisco, California, USA) via their API service. OpenAI processes this data using their GPT language models to generate transformed text based on your request.

User Permission

Before any data is shared with OpenAI, you must:

  • Grant "Full Access" keyboard permission in iOS Settings
  • Actively choose to use the keyboard extension to transform text
  • Accept this Privacy Policy

Data is only sent to OpenAI when you explicitly use the transformation features. No data is transmitted without your active use of the App.

Third-Party Data Protection

OpenAI maintains its own privacy practices and security measures. According to OpenAI's privacy policy:

  • API data is not used to train OpenAI's models
  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • OpenAI retains API data for a limited time for abuse monitoring, then deletes it

OpenAI's privacy practices provide equal or greater protection than our own policies. For complete details, review OpenAI's privacy policy at openai.com/privacy and their API data usage policy at openai.com/enterprise-privacy.

How We Use Your Data

  • Account Information: Used solely for authentication and account management
  • User Content: Sent to OpenAI's API only when you actively use transformation features. We do not store your text content after processing is complete
  • We do not sell, share with other third parties, or use your data for any purpose other than providing the text transformation service

How We Collect Data

  • Account Information: Collected directly from Apple or Google when you sign in using their authentication services
  • User Content: Collected when you actively select text and use the keyboard extension to transform it

Keyboard Extension Permissions

The App requires "Full Access" keyboard permission to function as a keyboard extension on iOS. This permission is necessary to send your text to OpenAI's API for processing. Without this permission, the App cannot provide text transformation services.

Data Storage

Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. We do not store your text content after it has been processed.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Control Over Data Sharing: You can stop sharing data with OpenAI at any time by not using the keyboard extension or by revoking "Full Access" permission in iOS Settings
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which will remove your email address from our systems
  • Data Access: Since we do not store your text content, there is no historical text data to access or delete

For questions about how OpenAI handles your data, please contact OpenAI directly or review their privacy policy.
