Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Introduction

ClarifierAI ("the App") is a keyboard extension for iOS that transforms text using GPT technology. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. Your privacy is our top priority.

Data We Collect

We collect the following information when you use ClarifierAI:

  • Account Information: Email address collected via Apple Sign-In or Google Sign-In for authentication purposes.
  • User Content: Text you submit for improvement, rephrasing, or translation is sent to OpenAI's API to process your request. This text is not stored by us after processing.

How We Use Your Data

  • Account information is used solely for authentication
  • User content is sent to OpenAI only to fulfill your request in real time
  • We do not sell, share, or store your text data

Third-Party Services

ClarifierAI uses OpenAI's API to process text. Please review OpenAI's privacy policy at openai.com/privacy.

Keyboard Extension Permissions

The App requires "Full Access" keyboard permission to function as a keyboard extension on iOS. This permission allows the App to send your text to OpenAI's API for processing.

Data Storage

Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. We do not store your text content after it has been processed.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
