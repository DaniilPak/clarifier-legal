Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Introduction

ClarifierAI ("the App") is a keyboard extension for iOS that transforms text using GPT technology. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. Your privacy is our top priority.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your data.

Specifically:

  • Text Processing: All text transformation happens locally on your device. The text you type or transform never leaves your device.
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any analytics data.
  • No User Accounts: The App does not require registration or user accounts.
  • No Third-Party Services: We do not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
  • No Network Requests: The App does not make any network requests or communicate with external servers.

Keyboard Extension Permissions

The App requires "Full Access" keyboard permission to function as a keyboard extension on iOS. However, despite this permission:

  • We do not access your typed text outside the keyboard interface
  • We do not log keystrokes
  • We do not transmit any data over the network

Data Storage

Any app preferences or settings are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
